Mage Progression Guide

Welcome to the Mage Progression Guide! This class is what's known as a glass cannon, meaning that it deals very high damage, at the cost of it being very fragile to when damage is done to you. But don't let that put you off! The mage class is very versatile, with its weapons having a variety of designs, making it a very flashy class.
